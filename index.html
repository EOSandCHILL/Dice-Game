Game Instructions

Welcome to The Dice Game!

Rules:

  1. Each player takes turns rolling the dice.
  2. If a player rolls a 1, they lose all their current round points and it becomes the next player's turn.
  3. If the player holds, they add their current round points to their total score.
  4. The first player to reach 100 points wins the game!

Good luck!
